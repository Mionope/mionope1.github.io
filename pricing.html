Big discounts and big winnings

  • Discounts: The shop can offer discounts on certain products or across the entire store. For example, a store might offer a 20% discount on all lipsticks for a week.
  • BOGO (Buy One Get One): The shop can offer a BOGO sale, where customers can buy one product and get a second one for free or at a discounted price.
  • Gift with purchase: The shop can offer a gift with purchase promotion, where customers receive a free gift with a minimum purchase. For example, a customer might receive a free makeup bag with any purchase over $50.
  • Clearance sale: The shop can offer a clearance sale, where they discount products that are being discontinued or have been overstocked.
  • Loyalty program discounts: The shop can offer discounts to members of their loyalty program. For example, a store might offer a 10% discount to members who have spent over $100 in the past year.
  • Flash sale: The shop can offer a flash sale, where they offer a limited-time discount on a specific product or range of products.
